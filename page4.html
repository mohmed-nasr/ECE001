Types of computer

  • Personal computer: - Small, individual, dependent on microprocessors
  • Workstations:-Powerful, single user, more powerful processors
  • Small computer:-multi-user, support hundreds at one time
  • Central Computer:-Powerful multi-user, supporting hundreds or thousands of users at one time
  • The supercomputer:-extremely fast that can execute hundreds of millions of instructions on the screen
